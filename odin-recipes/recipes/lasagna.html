Lasagna

<

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 16 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 12 ounces cottage cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 (12 ounce) package lasagna noodles

Directions

  1. Step1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step2: Cook ground beef, onion, and garlic in a large skillet over medium heat until the meat is browned.
  3. Step3: Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, and oregano and stir until well combined and heated through.
  4. Step4: Stir mozzarella, cottage cheese, and Parmesan together in a large bowl.
  5. Step5: Spoon a layer of the meat mixture onto the bottom of a slow cooker.
  6. Step6: Add a double layer of uncooked lasagna noodles, breaking noodles to fit into cooker as needed.
  7. Step7: Top noodles with a portion of cheese mixture.
  8. Step8: Repeat the layering of sauce, noodles, and cheese until all the ingredients are used.
  9. Step 9: Cover and cook on Low until lasagna noodles are tender, about 4 to 6 hours.
